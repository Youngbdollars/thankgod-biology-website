amazing biology lessons

🌱 plants are alive!

plants use sunlight to make their own food — that's called photosynthesis. they also give us oxygen to breathe.

the largest flower in the world is the rafflesia — it can grow 1 meter wide!

🦁 animal kingdom

animals are divided into groups: mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, and insects. mammals have hair or fur and feed milk to their babies.

a group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance" — so pink!

🧠 your amazing body

your heart beats about 100,000 times a day! the smallest bone is in your ear (stapes).

you have 206 bones, but babies are born with around 300 — some fuse together!

🐠 under the sea

the ocean is home to more than 200,000 known species. the blue whale is the biggest animal ever!

octopuses have three hearts and blue blood!

🌟 keep exploring — every day is a discovery!
